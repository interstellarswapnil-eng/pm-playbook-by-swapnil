---
title: Stakeholder management
---

## Treat it like a system
Stakeholder pain usually comes from:
- unclear ownership
- unclear decision rights
- surprises (timing, scope, outcomes)

## Cadence that works
- Weekly async update (always the same format)
- Monthly roadmap review
- Decision logs for contentious calls

## Good update format
- **Shipped**
- **In progress**
- **Next**
- **Risks / asks**

## One underrated move
Write down *who decides what* (and socialize it).
