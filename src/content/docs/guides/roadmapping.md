---
title: Roadmapping (without lying)
---

## A roadmap is a bet, not a promise
Use time horizons:
- **Now** (committed)
- **Next** (likely)
- **Later** (options)

## Keep it honest
- Show confidence levels (high/medium/low)
- Call out dependencies
- Separate goals from solutions

## What goes on the roadmap
- outcomes
- customer value
- a small number of initiatives

## What should *not* go on the roadmap
- every ticket
- exact dates without capacity math
