title: Prioritization (without politics)
---

## Two rules
1. **Make tradeoffs explicit** (what we’re *not* doing)
2. **Use a consistent scoring model** (even if it’s rough)

## Simple scoring model
Score each item 1–5:
- **Reach**: how many users/teams touched
- **Impact**: magnitude of improvement
- **Confidence**: evidence quality
- **Effort**: engineering + coordination

A quick heuristic: **(Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort**.

## What to document
- Assumptions
- Evidence links (tickets, calls, data)
- Why this now

## Anti-pattern
Prioritizing based on who shouted loudest last.
