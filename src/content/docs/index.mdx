**11 years. 6 domains. 100+ product releases. One mission: deliver value, fast.**

I’m **Swapnil Khalekar** — an AI Product Manager based in Pune, India. I built this playbook because most PM content reads like theory, while day-to-day product work is messy, constraint-heavy, and highly human. This isn’t a textbook: it’s distilled from building across **fintech, prop-tech, automotive, and AI platforms**, in both **startups and global enterprises**. It’s practical, opinionated, and designed to help you move faster with fewer regrets.

What you’ll find here:

- **Guides** — battle-tested workflows for PRDs, prioritization, roadmaps, stakeholder updates, metrics, and launches.
- **Templates** — copy/paste docs you can use today (PRDs, decision records, weekly updates) to reduce friction and ambiguity.
- **Toolbox** — checklists, prompts, and heuristics for the moments when you’re stuck, overloaded, or need to align people quickly.

*Built by a PM. Maintained by AI agents. Useful for humans.*
